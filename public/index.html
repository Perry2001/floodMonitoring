Flood Control System in Dagupan City

Our Mission:

Our mission is to safeguard the residents and communities of Dagupan City by providing accurate weather and water height estimates for each barangay (neighborhood). We strive to empower residents, local authorities, and emergency responders with real-time data to enhance flood preparedness, response, and overall safety.

Key Features:

Weather Monitoring: Our system constantly tracks and analyzes weather conditions to provide up-to-date weather forecasts, including rainfall predictions, temperature, and more. Water Height Estimations: We monitor water levels in every barangay, offering estimates that are vital for flood prediction and early warning. Tide Tables: Stay informed about tide schedules for Dagupan City throughout the week. This information is indispensable in managing coastal flood risks. Dam Updates: Get the latest updates on dams and reservoirs in the area, providing critical information for flood control and water resource management.

Contact Us:

We value your feedback and suggestions. If you have any questions, concerns, or ideas for improvement, please don't hesitate to contact us.
